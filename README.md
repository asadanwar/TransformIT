# TransformIT
Change Management System

Change Management System as a solution to optimize change management activities in your Department. 

The system is capable of the following: 
Tracking Emails from Exchange Server and Assigning Tickets.
Connecting to a Database, storing Information and Retrieving Information 

The following are also possible with the integration of a Database / Open Source System :
Change Management – Tracking of all change related activities 
Service Management – Tracking of all service requests to NSS Department with automated SLA calculations.
Disaster Recovery Mechanism – Automated backups of all data
Email Evidences  – All emails tracked with unique ID and available on request
Automated Scheduled Reporting – Individual tailor-made reports for relevant stakeholders
Business Intelligence – Ensures that Quality Assurance Projects are possible because of the wealth of information available, making new insights possible
Audits – Information Readily available for the auditors at a glance
Knowledge base  – each and every activity/change recorded as individual cases
Online Portal – Real time view of all activities in the network through an online portal
